Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions parents ask about admissions, fees, curriculum, and campus life.

What curriculum does St. M. S. Karamjot Model School follow?

We follow the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, ensuring a nationally recognized and structured approach to education from Nursery to Class 10.

What is the admission process?

Our admission process is simple — fill the online enquiry form or visit the school, submit required documents, attend a brief interaction, and receive your confirmation. The entire process is designed to be parent-friendly and stress-free.

What are the school fees?

Our fees are structured to be genuinely affordable while maintaining high educational standards. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Visit our Fees page for detailed information or contact the admissions office.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain an optimal student-teacher ratio to ensure individual attention for every child. Our class sizes are kept manageable to support personalized learning and regular progress monitoring.

Does the school provide transport?

Yes, we provide safe, GPS-tracked school buses covering all major routes. Each bus has a trained driver and a safety attendant. Parents can track the bus in real-time for complete peace of mind.

What are the school timings?

School hours are Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM. The administrative office is open from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM for parent enquiries and meetings.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and sibling discounts. Outstanding academic performers and students excelling in sports or extracurriculars may be eligible for fee concessions.

What safety measures are in place?

We have 24/7 CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, a visitor management system, fire safety equipment, first-aid facilities, and strict child safety protocols throughout the campus.

How can I schedule a campus visit?

You can book a campus visit through our website's contact form, call us directly, or WhatsApp us. We'll arrange a personalized tour at a time convenient for you.

What co-curricular activities are offered?

We offer a wide range including sports (cricket, basketball, athletics), arts (music, dance, painting), technology clubs, debating, yoga, and various inter-school competitions.
