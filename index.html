About Us

A Legacy of Trust, Learning & Growth

Established in 1993, St. M. S. Karamjot Model School has been a beacon of quality education in the community for over three decades. We believe that every child deserves access to excellent education, regardless of their family's financial background.

Our approach combines a rigorous CBSE-aligned curriculum with modern teaching methods, character development, and a safe, joyful learning environment. The result? Confident, capable young individuals ready to take on the world.

FAQ

Common Questions from Parents

Quick answers to help you make an informed decision about your child's education.

What is the admission age for Nursery?
Children who are 3 years old by March 31 of the admission year are eligible for Nursery. Age requirements vary by class — contact our admissions office for details.
What board is the school affiliated with?
St. M. S. Karamjot Model School follows a CBSE-aligned curriculum designed to build strong conceptual understanding and prepare students for future academic success.
What is the fee structure?
Our fees are designed to be affordable while maintaining premium education quality. Visit our Fees page or contact admissions for the detailed fee structure. There are no hidden costs.
Does the school provide transport?
Yes, we operate GPS-tracked school buses covering all major routes and nearby areas. Safety attendants are present on every bus.
What safety measures are in place?
Our campus has 24/7 CCTV surveillance, trained security staff, fire safety systems, and strict visitor protocols. Student safety is our top priority.
What extracurricular activities are offered?
We offer sports, music, dance, art, drama, debates, science club, and more. We believe in developing well-rounded individuals beyond textbooks.
