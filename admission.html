4

Receive your admission confirmation and welcome kit. You're part of the KMS family!

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate of the child
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Aadhar card of child and parents
  • Previous school TC (if applicable)
  • Previous year report card (Class 2+)
  • Address proof (utility bill / ration card)
