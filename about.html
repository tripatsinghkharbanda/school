Our Story

A Legacy Built on Trust & Excellence

Founded in 1993, St. M. S. Karamjot Model School began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality education accessible to every family. What started as a small school with a handful of dedicated teachers has grown into one of the most trusted educational institutions in the region.

Over 30+ years, we have nurtured thousands of young minds, guiding them toward academic excellence and personal growth. Our commitment has always been clear — provide the best possible education without placing an undue financial burden on families.

Today, with 1,500+ students, 80+ dedicated teachers, and a modern campus, St. M. S. Karamjot Model School stands as a testament to what happens when passion for education meets a commitment to affordability.
